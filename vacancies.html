Afrikaans First Additional Language Teacher

We are urgently looking for an Afrikaans Home Language Teacher..

Language to learners Grade 8 to 10

We are urgently looking for an Afrikaans Home Language Teacher who is able to teach Afrikaans First Additional Language to learners Grade 8 to 10:

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree with Afrikaans specialization and/or in the process of obtaining a degree.
  • Must be proficient in speaking, reading and writing in the language of instruction (Afrikaans).
  • SACE registered.
  • No criminal record.
  • Available for extra-curricular activities.
  • Computer literate
  • Effective member of a team.
  • Excellent organizational and administrative skills.


Complete the following document (available online) – “Application for Appointment”

Closing date: 05/02/2022
